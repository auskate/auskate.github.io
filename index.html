About me

I am a PhD student in the Vasishth Lab. My current interest is in predictive processing and what this tells us about how words are stored and accessed in the brain. More generally, I am interested in how different fields such as neuroscience, computer science and linguistics can inform each other to build comprehensive theories of human cognition.
