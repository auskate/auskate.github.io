Bayesian divergence point analysis of visual world data

April 8, 2021

Kate Stone

The visual world is a useful paradigm in psycholinguistics for tracking people’s eye fixations as they listen to sentences containing some kind of experimental manipulation. A common question is whether the experimental manipulation makes people fixate on a target object earlier in one condition versus another.
